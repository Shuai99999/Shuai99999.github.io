The landing page should be a group effort.

Each group member must be responsible for one content page.

The site should consist of a main landing page and at least one content page per group member (a total of 4 pages for most groups).

The index page
• The title of the site,
• A description of the site,
• Navigation links to each subpage,
• A distinct footer.

Each of your sub-pages should have:
• A header with the page title and a link back to the main page,
• Content broken into articles/sections (use your judgment to choose which semantic wrapper is better for your content) with their own headers and content,
• Internal links that will jump to each individual section (using links and ID attributes),
• At least one image,
• A table of data.

Please include at least 1 HTML element not covered in class per page.
